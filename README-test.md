# Steve L Recommends

Curated list of people, podcasts, YouTube channels, writers, and publications I personally enjoy and often recommend.

**Last Updated:** June 25, 2026

## Key People & Thinkers

**Konstantin Kisin**  
Co-host of *Triggernometry*. Excellent on free speech, culture, geopolitics, and current events.  
→ [X](https://x.com/KonstantinKisin) | [YouTube / Podcast](https://www.youtube.com/@triggerpod)

**Francis Foster**  
Co-host of *Triggernometry*. Thoughtful long-form conversations.  
→ [X](https://x.com/francisjfoster) | [YouTube / Podcast](https://www.youtube.com/@triggerpod)

**Ben Gilbert & David Rosenthal**  
Hosts of *Acquired* — one of the best business and technology history podcasts.  
→ [X](https://x.com/gilbert) | [Podcast](https://pca.st/podcast/eed80bc0-599b-0133-cb25-0d11918ab357)

**Jeffrey Lewis**  
Nuclear nonproliferation expert and host of *Arms Control Wonk*. Uses open-source intelligence.  
→ [X](https://x.com/ArmsControlWonk) | [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@armscontrolwonk)

**Dan Senor**  
Host of *Call Me Back*. Sharp geopolitical analysis, especially on Israel and the Middle East.  
→ [X](https://x.com/dansenor) | [Podcast](https://pca.st/podcast/d3ba2700-07dd-0139-41a2-0acc26574db2)

**Bari Weiss**  
Founder of *The Free Press*. Former *NYT* and *WSJ* journalist. Strong on culture and free speech.  
→ [X](https://x.com/bariweiss) | [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@thefreepress) | [The Free Press](https://www.thefp.com/)

**Lex Fridman**  
Long-form interviewer exploring science, technology, philosophy, and the human condition.  
→ [X](https://x.com/lexfridman) | [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@lexfridman)

**John Spencer**  
Urban warfare expert at West Point. Host of *The Urban Warfare Project*.  
→ [X](https://x.com/SpencerGuard) | [Podcast](https://pca.st/podcast/8bfe2910-0a72-0138-9f68-0acc26574db2)

**Walter Russell Mead**  
Historian offering deep historical context on geopolitics and international affairs.  
→ [X](https://x.com/wrmead) | [Podcast](https://pca.st/podcast/afaef4b0-fe30-013b-f55d-0acc26574db2)

## Podcasts

- **[Triggernometry](https://pca.st/podcast/f2902cd0-2c84-0136-c266-7d73a919276a)** — Konstantin Kisin & Francis Foster  
  Open, fact-based discussions on important and controversial issues.

- **[Acquired](https://pca.st/podcast/eed80bc0-599b-0133-cb25-0d11918ab357)** — Ben Gilbert & David Rosenthal  
  In-depth narrative deep dives into the history and strategy of the world’s greatest companies.

- **[Arms Control Wonk](https://pca.st/podcast/eed8f480-b393-0131-cde4-723c91aeae46)** — Jeffrey Lewis  
  Nuclear nonproliferation and global weapons programs using open-source intelligence.

- **[Call Me Back](https://pca.st/podcast/d3ba2700-07dd-0139-41a2-0acc26574db2)** — Dan Senor  
  Geopolitical and political analysis, with a focus on Israel and the broader Middle East.

- **[CAVASShips](https://pca.st/podcast/d0186f00-b039-0139-c1a5-0acc26574db2)** — Christopher Cavas & Chris Servello  
  Defense, maritime affairs, naval strategy, and shipbuilding.

- **[China Unscripted](https://pca.st/podcast/71565c80-66e3-0136-4e65-69745d675bc7)** — Chris Chappell  
  Candid conversations about Chinese politics, culture, and society.

- **[The Context](https://pca.st/podcast/d1993100-9c24-013c-a002-0acc26574db2)** — Kettering Foundation  
  Explores the complex story of American democracy.

- **[Goodfellows](https://pca.st/podcast/820bd3a0-584c-0138-97be-0acc26574db2)** — Bill Whan (Hoover Institution)  
  Deep historical and economic analysis of current events.

- **[Honestly](https://pca.st/podcast/20be1c20-ab7c-0139-c184-0acc26574db2)** — Bari Weiss  
  Culture, technology, ideology, and stories often overlooked by mainstream media.

- **[Intelligence Squared](https://pca.st/podcast/5bbc3e50-026e-0131-c9c5-723c91aeae46)**  
  World-class debates and discussions (Oxford-style format).

- **[The Lex Fridman Podcast](https://pca.st/podcast/78c58610-9061-0136-7b92-27f978dac4d)** — Lex Fridman  
  Long-form conversations on science, technology, philosophy, and more.

- **[Midrats](https://pca.st/podcast/db6b8e10-ed89-0130-4d3a-723c91aeae46)** — CDR Salamander & EagleOne  
  National security and military affairs.

- **[Reaganism](https://pca.st/podcast/4980f430-dd8a-0137-b6bf-0acc26574db2)** — Roger Zakheim  
  Contemporary issues through the lens of Reagan’s philosophy.

- **[Reasonable Doubt](https://pca.st/podcast/d4eb3560-13d8-0133-246a-059c869cc4eb)** — Mark Geragos  
  Legal analysis and true crime with a top criminal defense attorney.

- **[The Red Line](https://pca.st/podcast/d0862520-dc5c-0137-b658-0acc26574db2)** — Michael Hilliard  
  International relations and geopolitical trends.

- **[School of War](https://pca.st/podcast/be6e9a40-1299-013a-d58f-0acc26574db2)** — Aaron MacLean  
  Military history, statecraft, and great-power competition.

- **[Stop the World](https://pca.st/podcast/6294c980-de0c-013c-5d10-0acc26574db2)** — Australian Strategic Policy Institute  
  International affairs, defense, and security.

- **[The Urban Warfare Project](https://pca.st/podcast/8bfe2910-0a72-0138-9f68-0acc26574db2)** — John Spencer  
  Tactics and challenges of military operations in urban environments.

- **[What Really Matters](https://pca.st/podcast/afaef4b0-fe30-013b-f55d-0acc26574db2)** — Walter Russell Mead  
  Geopolitics with deep historical context.
